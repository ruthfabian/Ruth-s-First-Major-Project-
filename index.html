My name is Ruth Amaogechi Fabian; I am an Electronics Engineering student with a passion for software development, particularly in front-end technologies. As one of the 100 ladies selected for Glovo Women in Tech Bootcamp in partnership with She Code Africa and the Lagos State Government to learn Front-End Development, I am eager to deepen my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also gaining hands-on experience in creating user-friendly and visually appealing web interfaces. I look forward to learning industry best practices, improving my problem-solving skills, and building a strong foundation to advance my career in tech.
